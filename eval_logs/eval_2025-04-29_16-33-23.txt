User Prompt: what are the prerequisites for cdt406?
MDUBot Response: The prerequisites for CDT406 (Tillämpad artificiell intelligens) at Mälardalens universitet are as follows:

You need at least 120 hp of study credits, including programming skills in a high-level language, knowledge of system development/system design, and an understanding of several AI methods, with some at a detailed level. You also need to be familiar with how AI can be integrated into existing systems.

These prerequisites can be fulfilled by having completed courses such as Artificiell intelligens 7,5 hp or Lärande system 7,5 hp, or equivalent courses. 

Additionally, you must have Svenska B/Svenska 3 and Engelska A/Engelska 6. An exemption from the Svenska B/Svenska 3 requirement may be granted if the course is offered in English.
