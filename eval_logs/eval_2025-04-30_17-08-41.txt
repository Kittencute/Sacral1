Minst 120 hp vari ingår programmeringskunskaper i ett högnivåspråk, kunskaper i systemutveckling/systemdesign, förståelse för ett antal AI-metoder varav någon på djup och detaljerad nivå samt kunskaper i hur AI kan integreras i befintliga system.

MDUBot Response: The prerequisites for CDT406 (Tillämpad artificiell intelligens) at Mälardalens universitet are as follows:

You need at least 120 hp of education, including programming skills in a high-level language, knowledge of system development/system design, and an understanding of several AI methods, with some at a detailed level. You also need knowledge of how AI can be integrated into existing systems.

This can be fulfilled by having completed courses such as Artificiell intelligens 7,5 hp or Lärande system 7,5 hp, or equivalent courses.
